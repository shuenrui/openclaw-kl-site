About

    
Builders, not spectators.

    
Founded March 2026 in Kuala Lumpur, OpenClaw KL is the Malaysian chapter of Claw Collective — Impossible Finance's AI venture breeder. We're agent-agnostic: whatever tool gets your idea shipped, that's the right tool.

  

  

    

      
🦀

      
Zero to shipped

      
Bring a problem from your work or your neighbourhood — leave with a working agent that does it. Your first agent ships on your first night.

    

    

      
🌏

      
Regional by design

      
Part of Claw Collective's SEA network — community & events, our own products, and ambassadorships across three active chapters.

    

    

      
🏙️

      
Civic at heart

      
From kopitiam ops to city services — we build agents that make urban life work better, and open-source the playbooks.
